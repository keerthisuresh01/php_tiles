  • Tile Size :

    The tile size you pick can determine how big your room will look. Believe it or not, smaller tiles can make your room look smaller, while bigger tiles make your room look spacious. It is always preferred to use smaller tiles in areas like the kitchen and bathroom.

  • Colour :

    Ceramic flooring comes in various colour options. Pick a colour that goes well with the aesthetics of your home. A primary or neutral colour like a white ceramic tile can work well if you are going for simple ceramic tiles.

  • Room Size :

    The room size can make a significant difference in the tile you choose, so that is one factor you should consider before making the final decision. Depending on the size of your rooms, you can pick big or small ceramic tiles for living room and kitchen ceramic tiles that match your modular kitchen cabinets.

  • Finish :

    The options are unlimited when it comes to the finish and texture of ceramic tiles. From glazed ceramic tiles to ceramic wood tiles, we have everything you need. Pick a finish that will best suit the theme of your spaces, and the different variations of ceramic tiles will leave you amazed.
